Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-03

Who we are

Mara Akụkọ (the "Service"), also presented as PanAfroCore, is operated by Ikeniile Nexus, a company registered in Ireland under company number 820515 ("the Company", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

Information you give us

When you create an account we receive your email address, display name, and (if you sign in with Apple or Google) a profile photo from your identity provider. If you become a creator we collect the identity and banking information Stripe Connect requires to pay you out; Stripe collects that information directly and we never see your bank-account or tax-id numbers.

Information we collect as you use the app

We log the articles you read — including how long you spend and how far you scroll — the stories you save, share, and react to, the topics, sources, and creators you follow, the alerts you set, the comments and reports you submit, and the credits you spend. We use this to power your personalized For You feed, trust signals on sources and creators, and abuse prevention. Personalizing the For You feed is optional and off until you turn it on: you control it with the "Personalized For You feed" switch in Settings, and turning it off restores the newest-first feed and deletes the preferences we learned from your activity. We also collect crash reports through Sentry; crash reports are scrubbed of personal data before being sent.

How we use it

We use the information above to operate the app, to personalize what you see, to detect coordinated misinformation, to send you alerts you have asked for, to process payments and creator payouts, and to fight abuse. We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

We share data with: Auth0 (identity), Convex (database + function runtime), OpenAI (AI evaluations on articles and creator content), RevenueCat + Apple + Google (in-app purchases), Stripe (creator payouts), Cloudflare Stream (video hosting), Firebase (push notifications), and Sentry (crash reporting). Each provider is contractually bound to use the data only to deliver their service.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA / CPRA)

If you live in the EU or UK, GDPR gives you the right to access, correct, port, restrict, object to, and delete your personal data. If you live in California, CCPA / CPRA gives you the right to know what we have collected and to request deletion. You can delete your account and the personal data we hold by using "Delete account" in Settings. For other requests email privacy at panafrocore.com.

How long we keep it

Account data is retained while your account exists and for a short grace period after deletion (typically 14 days, so accidental deletions can be reversed) before being purged. Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics may be kept indefinitely.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has signed up, contact privacy at panafrocore.com and we will remove the account.

Changes

When we change this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, for material changes, notify you in the app before the changes take effect.
