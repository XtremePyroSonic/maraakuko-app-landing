Appeals Policy

Last updated 2026-06-03

What you can appeal

You may appeal an enforcement action taken against you — a content removal, account suspension, creator restriction or ban — and you may dispute a trust-score change. Submit an appeal from the relevant screen with a short statement explaining why.

How appeals are handled

Each appeal moves through a clear lifecycle: open → under review → granted or denied. A human reviewer evaluates it independently of the original decision and records the outcome and reason. You will not be penalized for filing a good-faith appeal.

Timelines

We aim to review appeals as quickly as possible. Granting an appeal results in the relevant action being reversed by a reviewer.
