Account & Data Deletion

Last updated 2026-06-03

How to delete your account

You can delete your Mara Akụkọ account and the personal data we hold at any time, directly in the app:

1. Open the app and make sure you are signed in.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Tap "Delete account".
4. Read the confirmation notice and confirm the request.

The 14-day grace period

When you confirm, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion 14 days later. During those 14 days you can cancel the request simply by signing back into the app. After the grace period ends, deletion runs automatically and cannot be reversed.

Deleting without access to the app

If you cannot sign in, email us from the address associated with your account at privacy at panafrocore.com with the subject "Account deletion request". We verify the request against your account email and complete deletion within 30 days, then confirm by email.

What we permanently delete

Deletion erases your sign-in identity and login credentials; your profile (display name, photo, interests and regions); your phone number, if verified; the comments, reactions and reports you submitted; your follows and blocks; your reading history, saved items and notification tokens; tips you sent; and, if you are a creator, your creator profile, drafts and uploaded media. Any remaining credit-wallet balance is forfeited and has no cash value. Security records tied to your account, such as hashed IP history, are also removed.

What we keep, and why

Articles you published as a creator remain available but are anonymized — authorship shows as "Deleted account" with no link back to you. Tips you received as a creator, and the related payment, payout and tax records held by our payment processors (Stripe and PayPal), are retained for the period required by tax, accounting and anti-fraud law. We may keep a minimal anonymized placeholder so public references such as comment threads do not break; it contains no data that identifies you.
